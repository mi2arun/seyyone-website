Remote Medical Scribe Services

Charting patient notes into EMR demands the need to have a medical assistant the whole time during the encounter of the patient with the physician so that each and every detail is entered real time. The absenteeism of medical assistant can make the ‘physicians’ day tough. On top of these, many patients do not consent to having an assistant physically present while they are being examined. Seyyone presents with two solutions which can make the notes charting process quite easier for the physician, while keeping it more patient-friendly.
