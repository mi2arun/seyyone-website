About Us

Seyyone has been offering healthcare back-office services for more than 25 years to various hospitals and clinic groups across the USA, Australia, Canada, Ireland and Germany

For over 25 years, Seyyone has been at the forefront of delivering comprehensive Healthcare Process Management and IT Solutions, blending deep domain expertise with technology-driven innovation. We serve as a trusted partner for healthcare providers, payers, and businesses across the globe, enabling operational excellence, regulatory compliance, and business transformation.

Founded with a clear vision to simplify the complexities of healthcare operations and business processes, Seyyone has grown into a multi-domain service provider. Our journey reflects an unwavering commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement.

Today, we combine the strength of skilled professionals, process rigor, and advanced technologies to offer a broad spectrum of services across Healthcare Services and Information Technology Solutions.

Seyyone, a HIPAA-compliant organization, offers it services from its own world-class infrastructure located at the heart of Coimbatore in India. Seyyone has grown over the years because of the support, trust and confidence from its delighted clients.

Seyyone is committed to relieving the clients from the pains and pressure of managing their back-office business processes and enabling them to concentrate on their core business activities.

Seyyone’s team of managers have expertise in understanding international clients’ requirements, and delivery the best services on time, with superior quality standards in accordance with the continuous advancements in technology, guidelines, and protocols and the new challenges they bring in.
